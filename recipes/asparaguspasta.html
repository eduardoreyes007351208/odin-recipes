Asparagus Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (8.8 oz.) package uncooked pappardelle pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped (about 1 tablespoon)
  • 1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into pieces
  • 3 tablespoons heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for serving
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high. Add pappardelle, and cook
    until al dente, about 6 minutes. Drain pasta over a heatproof bowl, reserving 1
    1/2 cups cooking water; set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a large, deep skillet over medium heat. Add shallot and garlic, and
    cook, stirring constantly, until softened, about 2 minutes. Add asparagus and 1/2
    cup of the reserved cooking water. Cook, stirring occasionally, until asparagus is
    bright green and tender, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add butter, cream, lemon zest and juice, salt, black pepper, crushed red pepper,
    and remaining 1 cup cooking water to skillet. Stir in cooked pasta, and cook over
    medium-low, gently stirring with tongs and sprinkling in Parmesan cheese, until
    sauce thickens and coats pasta, about 2 minutes. Divide pasta evenly among 4 bowls,
    and garnish with basil and additional Parmesan.
